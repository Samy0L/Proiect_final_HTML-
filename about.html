About Us

Our Mission & Beliefs

"Travel Dreams" is not just a travel agency; it is a workshop where wishes turn into reality. We believe every person carries at least one dream destination in their heart. Our mission is to turn those distant wishes into plane tickets and hotel reservations.

Our Core Goal: To offer the best vacations — in terms of quality and experience — at the best prices, ensuring your dream becomes accessible.

The "Travel Dreams" Philosophy: You don't need to be a millionaire to travel like royalty. We balance luxurious experience with smart budgeting.

Founding Story: Born From a Travel Journal

"Travel Dreams" was born from the vision of Bianca Dumitrescu, a former journalist specializing in stories about far-away cultures. For years, Bianca wrote about destinations many believed unreachable. She noticed a recurring pattern: people dreamed of traveling but abandoned the idea because they believed a perfect vacation was too expensive.

One evening, revisiting her old travel journal—filled with notes on smart, affordable ways to travel—she had an idea. If she could structure all that knowledge about off-hour flights, underrated boutique hotels, and hidden last-minute deals, she could make dream travel accessible for everyone.

In 2020, Bianca left journalism and launched "Travel Dreams," built on privileged access to information and a rigorous quality filtering system.

Our Vision Goals

  • Best Quality-to-Price Guarantee: Every package we offer achieves the best satisfaction score for its price.
  • Exclusive Partnerships: We aim to establish strategic partnerships offering discounts unavailable to the general public.
  • The Digital Dream Map: A feature allowing users to “save their dream destination” and receive alerts when the perfect offer appears.
  • Positive Local Impact: We support authentic tourism initiatives to ensure your trip benefits local communities.
